About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate and eccentric developer with a deep love for learning and creativity. As an ambivert and an INFP personality type, I find joy in both introspective projects and collaborative endeavors. My journey in software engineering has been driven by my insatiable curiosity and a desire to create innovative solutions that make a difference.

While pursuing my degree in software engineering, I've honed my skills in various programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Go, and Python. I have extensive experience using GitLab and GitHub for version control, gained through both academic and personal projects. My commitment to quality is evident in my ability to write high-quality, maintainable, and testable code.

Beyond the code, I have a strong appreciation for art, writing fiction, and exploring the fascinating world of AI. My goal is to become an AI developer in the future, and I am constantly seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills in this exciting field.

I believe in the transformative power of technology to address real-world challenges. My approach to problem-solving is grounded in thorough research, critical analysis, and an unwavering determination to deliver effective solutions. I excel in fast-paced environments and am committed to contributing to teams that value innovation and growth.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I hope to connect with you and explore how we can create something amazing together!
